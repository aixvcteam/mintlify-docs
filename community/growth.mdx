#### **Community Engagement**

* **User Engagement:** Early adopter rewards, incentivized feedback loops, and active participation in product testing.

* **Community Building:** Social media campaigns, influencer collaborations, and interactive AMAs to build trust and transparency.

**Referral Programs & Incentives:** Users earn platform benefits, such as trading fee discounts or premium feature access, through referrals and community contributions.
